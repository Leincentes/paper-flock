Privacy

Paper Flock production release · v1.2

Publisher and contact

Gamelo Studio publishes Paper Flock. Privacy and support questions may be sent to leincentes@gmail.com.

Information stored on your device

Paper Flock stores game progress, active puzzle state, preferences, tutorial completion, and recovery copies in your browser or installed web app. This information is used for offline play and exact resume.

Information sent by the game

Paper Flock has no account system, advertising SDK, behavioral analytics, location collection, purchase system, or automatic data upload. Static hosting providers may process ordinary request information when serving the game files.

Backups and deletion

Settings → Data can export a player backup, restore a backup, or reset all player data. Clearing the site's browser data or uninstalling the Home Screen app also removes locally stored information.

Children

Paper Flock does not knowingly request personal information from children. A parent or guardian should contact the publisher if they believe personal information was sent by email.
