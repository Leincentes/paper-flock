Accessibility

Paper Flock production release · v1.6.0

Paper Flock is designed for touch, pointer, keyboard, screen-reader, reduced-motion, high-contrast, and enlarged-text use.

Available controls

  • Keyboard activation and arrow navigation between active birds.
  • Visible focus indicators and a skip link to the puzzle board.
  • Screen-reader labels for bird position and direction.
  • Live announcements for blocked moves, hints, undo, and completion.
  • Large and extra-large text.
  • High contrast, reduced motion, and forced-color support.

Change accessibility preferences

Open Settings → Accessibility. Preferences remain on the current device and are included in player backups.

Accessibility support

Report an accessibility barrier to leincentes@gmail.com. Include the device, browser, and assistive technology when possible.
