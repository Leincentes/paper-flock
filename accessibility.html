Accessibility

Paper Flock public beta · Build v0.17

Paper Flock aims to support WCAG 2.2 Level AA practices for its public beta, while physical assistive-technology testing remains required before a conformance claim.

Supported interaction

  • Touch, pointer, and keyboard activation for puzzle controls.
  • Arrow-key movement between active bird controls.
  • Visible focus indicators and a skip link to the puzzle board.
  • Focus trapping and focus restoration for modal screens.
  • Polite screen-reader announcements for important game events.

Display preferences

The Accessibility settings panel provides larger text, higher contrast, and reduced decorative motion. Paper Flock also follows device reduced motion, increased contrast, and forced-color preferences.

Known limitations

The graphical board is labelled, but the complete puzzle relationship may still require additional screen-reader testing. Sound and haptics are optional and never the only source of gameplay information.

Report a barrier

Use the in-game Send feedback action and select Accessibility. Do not include personal or medical information. The report downloads locally and is not uploaded automatically.
