Open the test link provided by the creator.

This is an unfinished puzzle game. We are testing the game, not you. Play in
the way that feels natural. Small prompts will ask you to try sound and device
feedback. Anonymous timing, performance, and survey data stay on your device
until you choose to download the result. You may stop at any time.

At the end:
1. Complete the final questions.
2. Tap “Download result to send.”
3. Send the downloaded JSON file back to the creator.

Please do not enter your name, email address, phone number, or social handle.
