## Retroactive evaluation

The following values are reconstructed from existing v1.3 saves:

- completed campaign levels
- highest completed level
- campaign mastery records
- campaign feather total
- different Daily Flock dates
- unlocked paper-theme count
- minimum clean-completion count

Detailed move, hint, restart, deadlock, undo, launch, and replay totals begin
accumulating in v1.4 because earlier saves did not contain those counters.

## Progress visibility

Every locked achievement displays its current value and target. The Journal
does not conceal requirements, use random rewards, or require the player to
claim a reward before a deadline.
