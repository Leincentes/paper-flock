# Tester instructions

Thank you for testing Paper Flock. Please use the game naturally before reading
the detailed tasks.

1. Install from the private Google Play testing link.
2. Complete the tutorial without outside help.
3. Play at least five levels.
4. Close and reopen the game.
5. Turn on airplane mode and reopen it again.
6. Export a backup from Settings → Data, then restore it.
7. Continue toward Level 11 during the test period.
8. Report any startup recovery screen, lost progress, blocked controls,
   distorted layout, missing sound, or confusing instruction.

Do not share the private opt-in link publicly.
