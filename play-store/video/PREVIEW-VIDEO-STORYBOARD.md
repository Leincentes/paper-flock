# Google Play preview video storyboard

A final preview video must use real captured gameplay and be uploaded to
YouTube as public or unlisted, embeddable, not age-restricted, and with
monetization disabled.

## Recommended 24-second cut

| Time | Footage | On-screen caption |
|---|---|---|
| 0–3 s | Level opens; one bird has a clear path | Read the paths |
| 3–8 s | Bird escapes and neighboring birds rotate | Every move changes the flock |
| 8–13 s | A second move creates a dead end; Undo restores it | Learn, undo, try again |
| 13–18 s | Puzzle completion and feather result | Master cleaner solutions |
| 18–22 s | Achievement Journal and level map | Track 40 handcrafted puzzles |
| 22–24 s | Calm board with game title | Paper Flock |

## Capture requirements

- Record the actual v1.6.0 Android build at 1080×1920 or higher
- Show gameplay within the first 3 seconds
- Keep at least 80% of the video as real in-game footage
- Capture game sound, then add captions for muted autoplay
- Do not show a device frame, hands, ratings, awards, price claims, or
  “download/install/play now” calls to action
- Keep the final video concise; the first 30 seconds may autoplay muted
