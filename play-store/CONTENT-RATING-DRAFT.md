# IARC content-rating questionnaire draft

Answer the Play Console questionnaire truthfully based on the submitted build.

- Category: **Game**
- Violence: **None**
- Fear or horror: **None**
- Sexual content or nudity: **None**
- Profanity or crude humor: **None**
- Controlled substances: **None**
- Gambling or simulated gambling: **None**
- User-generated content: **None**
- User communication: **None**
- Location sharing: **None**
- Digital purchases: **None**
- Advertising: **None**

Expected result is a general-audience/low-age rating, but the rating authorities
make the final determination after submission.
