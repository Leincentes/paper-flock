## Full description

Paper Flock is a calm logic puzzle about reading paths and planning how each move changes the flock.

Tap an origami bird when its beak has a clear path to the edge. The bird escapes, while every touching bird folds clockwise. One simple action can open a route, close another, or leave the remaining flock trapped—so every move matters.

FEATURES

• 40 handcrafted campaign puzzles across two chapters
• One-touch rules that are easy to learn and increasingly strategic
• Three-feather mastery goals for players who want cleaner solutions
• A permanent Achievement Journal with transparent progress
• Daily Flock puzzles for optional variety, with no streaks or missed-day penalties
• Seven unlockable paper themes
• Undo, restart, and verified hints
• Sound and haptic feedback with accessible controls
• Local progress backup and restore
• Full offline play after installation

PLAYER-FIRST DESIGN

Paper Flock has no advertisements, energy system, loot boxes, forced waiting, paid progression, or push-notification pressure. Progress is stored locally on your device, and the game does not require an account.

Take a quiet break, study the folds, and find the path that frees the whole flock.
