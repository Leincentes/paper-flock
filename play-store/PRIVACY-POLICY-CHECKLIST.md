# Privacy policy publication checklist

Before Play review:

- [ ] Deploy `privacy.html` at the exact URL in
      `listing/en-US/privacy-policy-url.txt`
- [ ] Confirm the page loads without login
- [ ] Confirm it is readable without JavaScript
- [ ] Confirm it names **Paper Flock** and **Gamelo Studio**
- [ ] Confirm it explains local storage, export, deletion, and contact email
- [ ] Confirm it states that the Android app has no ads, analytics, accounts,
      purchases, or automatic uploads
- [ ] Confirm the same privacy page is reachable inside the app
- [ ] Confirm the URL returns HTTP 200 on mobile and desktop
