# Target audience draft

Recommended declaration for the current product:

- Target age groups: **13–15, 16–17, and 18+**
- Primarily directed at children under 13: **No**
- Designed to appeal to children through licensed child characters: **No**
- Ads: **None**

This is a product-positioning recommendation, not a way to avoid policy. Change
the declaration if Gamelo Studio intentionally markets the game to children
under 13. Selecting children as part of the target audience activates Google
Play Families requirements.
