# Play Console account registration handoff

The Play Console account must be created and owned by the publisher. Do not
register it under a contractor's identity.

## Publisher information prepared

- Public developer name: **Gamelo Studio**
- App name: **Paper Flock**
- Developer email: **leincentes@gmail.com**
- Package name: `com.gamelostudio.paperflock`

## Owner-only actions

The account owner must:

1. Sign in with the long-term publisher Google account.
2. Accept the Developer Distribution Agreement.
3. Pay Google's one-time registration fee.
4. Select Personal or Organization account type.
5. Provide and verify the legal identity and contact information requested by
   Google.
6. For an Organization account, provide organization verification information,
   including a D-U-N-S number when required.
7. Secure the account with multi-factor authentication.
8. Invite contractors through Users and permissions instead of sharing the
   owner password.

Do not upload the app until the permanent package name and account ownership
are confirmed.
