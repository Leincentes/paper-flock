Paper Flock Privacy Policy

Effective June 20, 2026 · production release v1.6.0

Publisher and contact

Gamelo Studio publishes Paper Flock. Privacy and support questions may be sent to leincentes@gmail.com.

Data stored on your device

Paper Flock stores campaign and Daily Flock progress, active puzzle state, achievements, lifetime player statistics, tutorial completion, paper-theme selection, sound and haptic preferences, and local recovery copies on your device. This information supports offline play, exact resume, and player-controlled backup and restore.

Data collection and sharing

The game has no account system, advertising SDK, behavioral analytics, crash-reporting SDK, location collection, purchase system, social network, or automatic gameplay upload. The Android app does not request Internet access and does not transmit player data off the device.

The public website host may process ordinary technical request information when serving this privacy page or the browser version of the game. Gamelo Studio does not use that hosting information to build player profiles.

Closed-test diagnostics

During closed testing, Paper Flock keeps a small rolling diagnostic log on the device. It may include startup and recovery results, level starts and completions, hints, undo actions, restarts, deadlocks, backup outcomes, and Android WebView renderer recovery. The log does not include raw save data, puzzle boards, account identifiers, advertising identifiers, contacts, location, or message content.

The diagnostic log is never uploaded automatically. A player may explicitly export a tester report from Settings → Data and then choose whether to share that file. The player can clear the log at any time.

Backups, transfer, and deletion

A player can explicitly export or restore a JSON progress backup using Settings → Data. Android operating-system cloud backup and device transfer are disabled for the app. Resetting player data in Settings or uninstalling the Android app removes its locally stored game data.

Device features

Optional sound and vibration feedback are processed on the device. Paper Flock does not request access to contacts, photos, videos, microphone, camera, location, notifications, or advertising identifiers.

Children

Paper Flock does not knowingly request personal information from children. The current Google Play product positioning is for players aged 13 and older. A parent or guardian may contact the publisher if they believe personal information was voluntarily sent by email.

Changes

Material changes to data practices will be reflected in this notice and in the Google Play Data safety section before the affected release is distributed.
