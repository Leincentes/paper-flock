# Paper Flock v1.4.4 — Google Play Preparation Report

## Decision

**READY FOR ANDROID AAB COMPILATION AND PLAY CONSOLE INTERNAL/CLOSED TESTING.**

The repository now contains a native Android wrapper, Play Store listing
materials, policy drafts, graphics, a closed-test plan, and a signed-AAB GitHub
Actions workflow. A production release is not yet approved because the AAB must
still compile on an Android SDK runner, be uploaded by the account owner, pass
Google Play's automated checks, and complete physical-device closed testing.

## Product changes in this candidate

- Sound remains enabled by default for new players and full-reset players.
- Existing sound preferences are preserved.
- The first Level 11 deadlock now presents a one-time recovery lesson.
- That first lesson does not add a deadlock penalty.
- Subsequent deadlocks keep the existing rules and penalties.
- Save schema remains version 12.

- Package name: `com.gamelostudio.paperflock`

- Minimum SDK: 24
- Target/compile SDK: 36
- Android Gradle Plugin: 8.13.2
- Gradle: 8.13
- JDK: 17
- AndroidX WebKit: 1.16.0
- Permissions: `android.permission.VIBRATE` only
- Internet permission: not requested
- Ads, analytics, billing, accounts, notifications, and remote configuration:
  not included

The app serves verified local web assets through `WebViewAssetLoader` at the
HTTPS-compatible `appassets.androidplatform.net` origin. Service-worker
registration is skipped inside the Android wrapper because all runtime assets
are already bundled locally. External policy and support links open in the
user's chosen external app.

Android cloud backup and device-transfer backup are disabled. Players retain
the explicit JSON export/import mechanism through Android's document picker.

## Closed-test experiment

The test cohort should recruit 15 participants and, when the account is subject
to Google's new-personal-account gate, maintain at least 12 opted-in testers for
14 consecutive days.

The product experiment measures whether the Level 11 recovery lesson helps
players understand rotation consequences, use Undo, complete the level, and
continue voluntarily.

## Release gate

Paper Flock v1.4.4 may enter internal and closed testing after:

- the GitHub Android workflow passes;
- the generated signed AAB installs through Play;
- the deployed privacy URL shows the v1.4.4 policy;
- no critical issue appears in Play's pre-launch report.

Production approval remains blocked until the closed-test and physical-device
acceptance criteria are complete.
